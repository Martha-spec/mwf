Mayondo Wood and Furniture (MWF) is a proudly Ugandan-owned company. We connect tradition with innovation by supplying top-quality wood (timber, poles, hardwood, softwood) from rural areas to construction companies and furniture dealers.

We also make furniture in our workshop — beds, sofas, dining tables, and office furniture. Every item is crafted with care and designed to last.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver quality products and support local communities, bridging the gap between rural supply and urban demand with honesty and care.

How We Work

We record every sale carefully — product name, customer name, quantity, date, and payment type (cash, cheque, or bank). A 5% transport fee is added if we handle delivery. Our team of 8 staff handles stock and loading.

Why Choose Us

  • Reliable upcountry wood suppliers
  • Quality, handcrafted furniture
  • Friendly and efficient team
  • Transparent prices and clear records
  • Regular stock and sales reports

Whether you're building, furnishing, or restocking — Mayondo Wood & Furniture is here to serve you with strength, skill, and style.
